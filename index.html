OUR JOURNEY

It Started With A Dream

Five years ago, Coded Clouds didn’t start as a company, it started as a frustration. Muhammad Hashim was staring at the same problem every small business in Pakistan complained about undocumented economy, the cash-only chaos, the “bhai, likh diya hai” style bookkeeping that kept half the country invisible.

He wasn’t a giant corporation, he wasn’t backed by investors just one young guy with a laptop, a vision, and that stubborn “I’m gonna fix this someday” energy.

So he built Coded Clouds. Not to chase hype. Not to become another copy-paste software house. The mission was simple: earn enough capital, skills, and team strength to eventually launch Pakistan’s first real fintech solution tailored for small businesses the dukaan owners, mechanics, mini wholesalers, freelancers, the backbone of the undocumented economy.

But he knew you can’t launch a revolution on empty pockets. So Coded Clouds started with what was possible: web development, app development, custom CRM builds, PMS systems anything that sharpened the team and pushed the company forward.

Five years later, Coded Clouds isn’t just a software house anymore. It’s a quiet movement. A team built around the same vision Hashim started with: Make Pakistani businesses visible. Make fintech accessible.

And the journey hasn’t peaked yet, this is just the warm-up before the real product drops.
